Founding Story

Fanwings was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create a one-stop shop for all air movement needs. Founded in 2024 by three passionate engineers dedicated to sustainability and innovation, our journey began with a vision to tackle the common challenges associated with air movement.

We noticed a significant gap in the Indian market for high-efficiency products, leading to discomfort and inefficiency. With a small, dedicated team of like-minded professionals, we set out to develop advanced air movement technologies that enhance comfort while minimizing energy consumption. Our mission is to revolutionize the way air is managed in homes and businesses, providing solutions that prioritize both performance and sustainability.
